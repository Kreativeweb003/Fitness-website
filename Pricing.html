Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and plans

Can I switch plans later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. We'll prorate the difference so you only pay for what you use.

Are there any cancellation fees?

No cancellation fees. You can cancel anytime with 30 days notice. We'll refund any unused portion of your membership.

Do you offer family or group discounts?

Yes! We offer special pricing for couples (15% off) and families (20% off). Contact our support team for custom pricing options.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, and direct bank transfers. All payments are securely processed.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes! We offer a 7-day free trial for all new members. Experience our facilities and training approach before committing.
