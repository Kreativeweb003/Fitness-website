Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our programs

How do I get started with a personal trainer?

Getting started is simple! Schedule a free consultation with one of our fitness experts. During this session, we'll discuss your goals, assess your current fitness level, and create a personalized plan tailored to your needs.

What should I bring to my first training session?

For your first session, please bring comfortable workout clothes, athletic shoes, a water bottle, and a towel. We provide all necessary equipment. Also, come prepared to discuss your fitness history and any health concerns.

Can I change my training program later?

Absolutely! We regularly reassess your progress and adjust your program accordingly. As your fitness level improves or your goals evolve, we'll modify your training to ensure you continue making progress.

Do you offer nutrition guidance?

Yes, all our programs include basic nutritional guidance. For more comprehensive nutrition planning, we offer personalized meal plans created by certified nutritionists as part of our Pro and Elite packages.
